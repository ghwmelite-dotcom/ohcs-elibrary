## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [ohcs-elibrary.pages.dev](https://ohcs-elibrary.pages.dev)
2. Click **Register** or **Get Started**
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Email**: Use your official .gov.gh email address
   - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters with letters and numbers
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Department/MDA**: Select your ministry, department, or agency
4. Click **Create Account**
5. For .gov.gh emails: Your account is automatically verified
6. For other emails: Check your inbox for verification email

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. If you have 2FA enabled, enter your authentication code
4. Click **Sign In**

### Forgot Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for the reset code (or view the on-screen code for government emails)
4. Enter the 6-digit code
5. Create a new password

## Document Library

The document library is your central repository for all official documents.

### Browsing Documents

1. Navigate to **Library** in the main menu
2. Browse by:
   - **Categories**: Policies, Circulars, Guidelines, Reports, etc.
   - **MDAs**: Filter by specific ministry or department
   - **Document Type**: PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint
   - **Date**: Recent, This Month, This Year, All Time

### Searching Documents

1. Use the search bar at the top of the library
2. Enter keywords, document title, or content
3. Apply filters to narrow results:
   - Category
   - MDA
   - Date range
   - Document type

### Viewing Documents

1. Click on any document card to open it
2. Use the built-in PDF viewer to read
3. Available actions:
   - **Download**: Save to your device
   - **Bookmark**: Save for quick access later
   - **Share**: Share link with colleagues
   - **AI Summary**: Get AI-powered summary

### Uploading Documents

1. Click **Upload Document** button
2. Drag and drop your file or click to browse
3. Fill in document details:
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive title
   - **Description**: Brief summary
   - **Category**: Select appropriate category
   - **Tags**: Add relevant keywords
4. Click **Upload**
5. Document enters review queue (if required)

### Bookmarking Documents

1. Click the bookmark icon on any document
2. Access your bookmarks from **My Bookmarks** section
3. Organize bookmarks into collections

## AI-Powered Features

### Document Summarization

1. Open any document
2. Click **AI Summary** button
3. Receive:
   - **Executive Summary**: 2-3 paragraph overview
   - **Key Points**: Bullet-point highlights
   - **Action Items**: Identified tasks or requirements

### Smart Search

The AI-powered search understands natural language:
- "Show me policies about leave entitlements"
- "Find circulars from 2024 about promotions"
- "Documents related to salary adjustments"

### Document Analysis

Request in-depth analysis:
1. Open a document
2. Click **Analyze with AI**
3. Choose analysis type:
   - **Compliance Check**: Compare against regulations
   - **Summary Report**: Detailed breakdown
   - **Key Changes**: Compare with previous versions

## Community Forums

Engage with colleagues across the civil service.

### Forum Categories

- **General Discussion**: Open conversations
- **Policy Questions**: Ask about policies and procedures
- **Best Practices**: Share successful approaches
- **Technical Help**: IT and system-related questions

1. Navigate to **Forum**
2. Select appropriate category

4. Enter:

   - **Content**: Detailed post with context

1. Click the **Follow** button on any topic
2. Receive notifications for new replies
3. Access followed topics from your profile

## Chat & Messaging

Connect with colleagues in real-time.

### Direct Messages

1. Click **Chat** in the main menu
2. Click **New Message**
3. Search for a colleague by name or email
4. Start your conversation

### Group Chats

1. Click **New Group Chat**
2. Add members (search by name)
3. Give the group a name
4. Start chatting

### Chat Features

| Feature | How to Use |
|---------|------------|
| **Text Messages** | Type and press Enter or click Send |
| **Voice Messages** | Click microphone icon, record, send |
| **File Sharing** | Click attachment icon, select file |
| **Emojis** | Click emoji icon to open picker |
| **GIFs** | Click GIF icon to search and send |
| **Reactions** | Hover message, click reaction icon |

### Voice Messages

1. Click the microphone icon
2. Hold or click to start recording
3. Speak your message
4. Release or click again to stop
5. Preview and send

### Managing Chats

- **Pin Chat**: Keep important conversations at the top
- **Mute**: Disable notifications for specific chats
- **Archive**: Hide old conversations
- **Search**: Find messages within conversations

## Groups & Collaboration

Create and join professional groups.

### Group Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Public** | Anyone can view and join |
| **Private** | Visible to all, join requires approval |
| **Secret** | Only members can see the group exists |

### Creating a Group

1. Navigate to **Groups**
2. Click **Create Group**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Name**: Group name
   - **Description**: Purpose of the group
   - **Type**: Public, Private, or Secret
   - **Cover Image**: Optional banner image
4. Click **Create**
5. Invite members

### Group Features

- **Posts**: Share updates, files, and ideas
- **Comments**: Discuss posts with other members
- **Events**: Schedule group events or meetings
- **Files**: Shared document repository
- **Members**: View and manage group members

### Group Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control, can delete group |
| **Admin** | Can manage members and posts |
| **Moderator** | Can moderate content |
| **Member** | Can post and comment |

### Posting in Groups

1. Open the group
2. Click in the post composer
3. Write your post
4. Add attachments (images, files, GIFs)
5. Click **Post**

## Gamification & Rewards

Earn recognition for your contributions.

### XP (Experience Points)

Earn XP through various activities:

| Activity | XP Earned |
|----------|-----------|
| Daily login | 10 XP |
| Upload document | 50 XP |
| Create forum topic | 25 XP |
| Reply to topic | 10 XP |
| Receive upvote | 5 XP |
| Complete profile | 100 XP |

### Levels

Progress through levels as you earn XP:

| Level | XP Required | Title |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | 0 | Newcomer |
| 2 | 100 | Contributor |
| 3 | 500 | Active Member |
| 4 | 1,500 | Engaged Citizen |
| 5 | 3,000 | Knowledge Seeker |
| 6 | 6,000 | Expert |
| 7 | 10,000 | Senior Expert |
| 8 | 15,000 | Master |
| 9 | 25,000 | Grand Master |
| 10 | 50,000 | Legend |

### Badges

Unlock badges for achievements:

- **Early Bird**: Log in 7 consecutive days
- **Bookworm**: Read 50 documents
- **Helpful**: Receive 100 upvotes
- **Contributor**: Upload 10 documents
- **Social Butterfly**: Join 5 groups
- **Chatterbox**: Send 500 messages
- **Knowledge Sharer**: Create 25 forum topics

### Leaderboard

1. Navigate to **Leaderboard**
2. View rankings:
   - This Week
   - This Month
   - All Time
3. Filter by MDA or category
4. See your position and progress

### Streaks

Maintain daily login streaks:
- 7-day streak: Bronze badge
- 30-day streak: Silver badge
- 100-day streak: Gold badge

## FAQ

### Account & Access

**Q: Who can create an account?**
A: Any civil servant with a valid .gov.gh email, or with approval for other emails.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page. A reset code will be sent to your email or displayed on screen.

**Q: How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication?**
A: Go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app.

### Documents

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX (max 50MB).

**Q: My document is not appearing. Why?**
A: It may be pending approval. Check with an administrator.

**Q: Can I edit an uploaded document?**
A: You can update document metadata. To change the file, upload a new version.

### Forum & Chat

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Click the three-dot menu on any post or message and select "Report."

**Q: Can I delete my messages?**
A: Yes, click the delete icon on your own messages (within 24 hours).

### Gamification

**Q: How is XP calculated?**
A: XP is awarded for various activities. See the Gamification section for details.

**Q: Can I lose XP?**
A: XP is never removed, but some activities may be limited to prevent abuse.
